## Quick Tips

### While Downloading:
1. **Size**: Download "Large" or "Original" size
2. **Format**: JPG is fine for all photos
3. **Naming**: Save with exact filename immediately
4. **Location**: Save directly to `/public/images/` folder

### What to Look For:
- ✅ Natural, warm lighting
- ✅ Neutral, earthy colors
- ✅ Authentic, handmade aesthetic
- ✅ Clear, high-quality images
- ❌ Avoid harsh lighting
- ❌ Avoid bright, artificial colors
- ❌ Avoid overly commercial/stock look

### Color Palette Reference:
- Forest Green: #2F4F3E
- Sage Green: #8FAE9A
- Ivory: #F7F5F0
- Linen Beige: #D2C4B2

## Troubleshooting

**Image not showing on website?**
- Check filename matches exactly (case-sensitive)
- Check file is in `/public/images/` folder
- Clear browser cache and refresh
- Check browser console for errors

**Image looks pixelated?**
- Download larger size from source
- Ensure original is at least 1920px wide

**Image file too large?**
- Use TinyPNG to compress
- Target under 500KB per image
