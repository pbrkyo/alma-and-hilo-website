## Image Guidelines for Replacement Photos

When replacing with actual brand photos, maintain these specifications:

### Technical Requirements
- **Format**: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920px on longest side
- **File Size**: Optimize to under 500KB per image
- **Color Profile**: sRGB

### Aesthetic Guidelines
- **Lighting**: Natural, warm lighting preferred
- **Colors**: Match brand palette (forest green, sage, ivory, linen beige)
- **Style**: Authentic, artisan, handmade aesthetic
- **Mood**: Warm, intimate, traditional craftsmanship

### Specific Image Needs

#### Hero Lifestyle (`hero-lifestyle.jpg`)
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 (portrait)
- Subject: Person wearing Alma & Hilo crochet piece
- Setting: Natural outdoor or indoor setting with good lighting
- Mood: Lifestyle, aspirational, natural

#### Mother-Daughter (`mother-daughter.jpg`)
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 (portrait)
- Subject: María and Sofía together (founders)
- Setting: Workshop, home, or natural setting
- Mood: Intimate, authentic connection, warmth

#### Product Images (`product-*.jpg`)
- Aspect ratio: 4:5 (portrait)
- Subject: Individual product, well-lit
- Setting: Clean background or lifestyle setting
- Mood: High-quality, artisan, detailed

#### Process Images (`hands-crochet.jpg`, `yarn-materials.jpg`)
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (square)
- Subject: Close-up of process or materials
- Setting: Natural workspace
- Mood: Authentic craftsmanship, detail-oriented

## Photography Tips

### For DIY Brand Photography:

1. **Lighting**
   - Use natural window light when possible
   - Shoot during golden hour (early morning or late afternoon)
   - Avoid harsh overhead lighting

2. **Composition**
   - Use rule of thirds
   - Leave negative space for text overlay if needed
   - Keep backgrounds simple and uncluttered

3. **Styling**
   - Match brand color palette
   - Include natural elements (plants, wood, natural fabrics)
   - Keep styling minimal and authentic

4. **Equipment**
   - Modern smartphone cameras are sufficient
   - Use portrait mode for depth of field
   - Consider a simple ring light for indoor shots

### For Professional Photography:

If hiring a photographer, share:
- This README
- The STOCK-IMAGES-GUIDE.md file
- Brand color palette and aesthetic guidelines
- Examples of current placeholder images for reference

## Image Optimization

Before adding final images to the website:

1. **Resize** to appropriate dimensions (see guidelines above)
2. **Compress** using tools like:
   - TinyPNG (https://tinypng.com)
   - Squoosh (https://squoosh.app)
   - ImageOptim (Mac)
3. **Test** on website to ensure quality is maintained
4. **Backup** original high-resolution versions
